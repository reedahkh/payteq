FEATURES

Payteq has a host of features that makes it your HR and payroll solution of choice in today's enterprise where compliance, convenience and safety are of paramount importance.

It is easy to use

Payteq helps reduce the time and effort required to administer payroll every month. Now you can:

  1. Easily calculate deductions and additions.
  2. Automate the approval process.
  3. View payment history and detailed reports.
  4. Easily bring new employees on-board.

transform

It is Flexible

Payteq is designed and optimized for a rich user experience across devices and browsers. So you get to enjoy the same level of ease, speed and functionality from anywhere at anytime. It is a web based system with built in flexibility and customisable options to ensure that you can:

  1. Automatically adjust computations.
  2. Administer or approve payroll anytime, anywhere.

It is Safe

Payteq runs on the latest web technologies to provide an intuitive and highly secure online application. We ensure your data is safe, secure and accessible 24/7. Payteq is equipped with:

  1. SSL Security
  2. Encrypted User Passwords.
  3. Auto Log out
  4. Regular Scheduled Backups
